A Better RTX-based ray tracing shader that aims to make your gaming experience much more realistic and natural, with smooth lighting transitions, realistic Rayleigh scattering, and calm water waves.

**Features:**

- Burley diffuse BRDF
- Entity emissive support
- Rain wetness and puddles
- Custom sun light
- Realistic Rayleigh scattering
- Realistic night sky
- Calm water waves
- ACES tonemapping with 6000K color temperature
